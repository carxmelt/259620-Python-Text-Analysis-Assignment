# My-Python-Text-Analysis-Assignment

## Short summary:

I chose the ECB Press Conference from 5 June 2025 for this text analysis assignment. This press conference was about the European Central Bank’s decision to cut interest rates to support the economy. It explained that inflation was decreasing and moving closer to the 2% target, but there was still uncertainty about future economic conditions. The ECB noted that economic growth was relatively weak, which was why they were easing monetary policy. The ECB was carefully balancing between controlling inflation and supporting economic growth. It was interesting to read about the decisions they made and to think about why these decisions were made.

Next, for my sentiment analysis, I used the TextBlob sentiment package. I chose this because it is beginner-friendly and easy to implement. TextBlob already has a built-in sentiment analysis that provides both polarity and subjectivity scores. It also works quite fast to analyze paragraphs compared to other sentiment packages such as Transformers. The accuracy is also good.

For the results, my paragraph-level sentiment analysis suggests that the tone of the press conference is largely neutral to positive, with almost no negative tone. Out of 52 paragraphs, 31 paragraphs are classified as positive, 20 paragraphs are neutral, and only 1 is negative. This shows that the ECB conveyed some optimism about economic developments while presenting the information in a factual manner.

According to the word cloud results, the most frequent words include “rate,” “interest,” “target,” “growth,” and “economy.” This suggests that the main focus of the press conference is on monetary policy, particularly interest rate decisions and the ECB’s inflation target. Additionally, it indicates that the discussion also covers topics such as the economic outlook.
